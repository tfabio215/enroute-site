Who We Are

Always moving
forward

Founded in 1986, Enroute Imports Inc. has grown from a specialty oil importer into one of Canada's most trusted edible oil suppliers — operating from a modern HACCP/GMP Kosher-certified facility in Mississauga, Ontario.

We supply the full range — Canola, EVOO, Sunflower, Soybean, Corn, Non-GMO, and Organic oils — to retailers, food service operators, manufacturers, and private label programs nationwide and around the world.
